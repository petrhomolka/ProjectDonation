    ABOUT

    Own a piece of internet history!

    Each one of these billboards is a unique NFT. NFT is a modern new approach to how to own digital things. In this case the thing is the billboard that could be bought and potentially sold later on on a NFT market.

    The buyer will receive the NFT of the bought billboard in his NFT wallet that will assure he is the owner. After the billboard is bought the owner can change it's content (more in rules).

    There will be all the time only 100 of NFTBillboards shown above in the grid.

    RULES

    Owner is allowed to change the billboard's content once after he bought it. It is true also in case of rebuy later on between users.

    Another possible way, to change the billboard's content, is buying an NFT called "Change Ticket". If the owner wants to change the billboard's content the NFT called "Change Ticket" has to be transferred to this website owner. After that the billboard's owner is allowed to do the change.

    The Change Ticket could be also sold to someone in the same way as NFTBillboard.

    The content of the billboard has to be provided in the form of a png image with size 100px to 70px.

    The content of the billboard will be changed latest in 10 days after email with signed message and png file will arrive. This is true only if rules written above were fulfilled.

    Billboards could be combined to create one bigger picture. Each one is still managed separately but due to lack of borders, it is possible to create a big uninterrupted billboard image.

    How to send content change request?

    Since NFTBillboards doesn't store any identification data, we need to rely on other methods to verify that you are the legitimate owner of the NFTBillboard. The only way to do this securely is to use a private key to sign a message.

    • Step1: Go to https://app.mycrypto.com/sign-message Double check the URL and SSL certificate.
    • Step2: Choose one of the options for linking your wallet. Best choice depends on your used wallet. For some of them are integrated interfaces, for other ones you will have to use Mnemonic Phrase.
    • Step3: Type your message in the Message box and then select Sign Message. Content of message has to be:

      "I request to change the content of my billboard number XX to the attached image. MM/DD/YYYY"

      Where XX is the number of your NFTBillboard as mentioned in the name of your NFT.
    • Step4: Please copy the full generated message including the opening and closing brackets to your clipboard and paste it to an email. Don't forget to attach the required image you want to have as the content of your NFTBillboard. More in the rules for content.
    • Step5: Sent everything to the address written in the lock property of your NFT Billboard.
